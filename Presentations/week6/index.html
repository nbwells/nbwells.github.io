This website layout allows Larry's potential customers to easily navigate the site, meaning that they will be able to find vehicles and the wanted information quickly, in turn leading to more sales for Larry the Leaser.

I used the images to work as links to create visual interest in the navigation, which customers may ignore, as phones have made us used to scrolling rather than clicking. The large images encourage customers to dig deeper into his site, exploring important information. This also adds a visual element, and makes the site more interesting.

Because some of the most important elements of a car leasing site would be the cars, and location of the business, I wanted to ensure that the cars available were emphasized through their own link with an image. This increases the functionality of the website.
