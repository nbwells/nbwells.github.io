Color Palettes for Sugar Smash

Analogous

This colour scheme uses blue to create a calming mood. By looking at the site you might assume the game is about serenity, one a person plays when looking for something to help them relax.

Complimentary

This colour scheme excudes fun and excitement. The vibrant hues of green and pink can be associated with happiness, imagination and fun. By looking at the site you might assume the game is targetted at younger audience who is full of energy.

Tetrad

This colour scheme brings mixed emotions. The contrast between the yellow and blue-green colour plays between feelings of warm groundedness and feelings of new and excitement. By looking at the site you might assume the game is targetted at someone spontaneous or wacky.
